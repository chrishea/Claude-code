Discover Cloudcroft

Nestled at 8,663 feet in the Sacramento Mountains, Cloudcroft is a charming mountain village offering cool summer escapes, snowy winters, and year-round adventure. From hiking trails to local breweries, historic lodges to community events, there's always something happening on the mountain.
